What is FSW?

What’s better than a good classical 3-day-long forró dance festival in some beautiful city in a foreign country? That’s right, this memorable, weeklong floating forró festival on several yachts, sailing in well-tempered, crystal-clear waters.

Throughout the day, its sailing, swimming, snorkeling, kite surfing, stand up paddling, yoga, chilling on floaties, sunbathing on deck. We have lots of time for talking, relaxing and dancing with fellow forrózeir@s from all around the world, exploring small coastal towns, dreamlike islands and beautiful beaches and on rare occations even dolphin watching.

In the evening we have open pot dinners on the boats, group-strolls through a nearby town and forró workshops in special locations like abandoned building sites, lonely beaches, vivid plazas and nautical landing stages.

In the night we have forró dance parties (both on- and offshore), caipirinhas, stargazing under the clearest midnight sky, special themed dance parties, on some occasions a night swim through bioluminous glowing plankton.
